Terengganu Travel Tips

Plan your trip easily and enjoy an unforgettable experience in Terengganu.

Best Time to Visit

Weather and Seasons

The best season to visit is the dry season between March to September suitable for beach activities

Seasons Event

Monsoon Cup regatta, Ramadan celebrations and traditional festivals.

Warning

Avoid monsoon season for beach activities because the sea is rough.

Transportation Options

To Terengganu Options such as airplane (flight to Sultan Mahmud Airport), express bus, or self-drive.
Within Terengganu Local bus services, taxis, e-hailing, and ferry boats to islands such as Pulau Redang or Perhentian.

Culture and Ethics

Clothes

Respect the local culture by wearing modest clothing, especially in public places and mosques.

Interaction

Speak in a soft and polite tone

Warning

Do not speak rudely and damage public property.

Safety and Packaging

Safety

Always keep important documents safe and take precautions if doing water activities.

Recommended Items to Bring

Sunscreen lotion, light clothing, trekking shoes, and mosquito repellent.

Accommodation

Hotel, Homestay, or Chalet.
